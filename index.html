Professional Life

With hands-on experience in roles like Teaching Assistant, Research Assistant, and Junior Data Scientist, I've developed expertise in fine-tuning models, creating predictive analytics solutions, and building real-time data dashboards that drive strategic insights.

I'm ready to bring my technical skills, problem-solving mindset, and passion for innovation to a forward-thinking team, helping to drive meaningful change and deliver measurable results.
